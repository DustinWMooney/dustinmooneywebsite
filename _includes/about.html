About Dustin

Dustin works in client experience, marketing, and communications. He has a background in politics working for a variety of candidates and non-profits. In his free time he enjoyes time with family, playing chess and golf, watching movies, and rock climbing.

Graduating from the University of Central Florida with a degree in Political Science. He especially enjoyed studying intelligence, political economy, and creative writing. After graduating Dustin set off to do aid work in Africa, working with orphans.

He also attended Bishop McLaughlin Catholic for high school. In his free time he enjoys spending time with his wife and puppy, Maverick.
